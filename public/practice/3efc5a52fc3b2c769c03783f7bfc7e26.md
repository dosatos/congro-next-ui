
## Ride-sharing Platform

**Question:** Design a ride-sharing platform like Uber

**Hints:**
- Consider using geospatial indexing for efficient driver-rider matching
- Think about handling real-time location updates from millions of users
- Explore options for implementing dynamic pricing based on demand
- Consider how to handle payment processing and driver payouts
- Think about implementing a rating system for both riders and drivers

> #### Key Considerations:
> - Real-time matching of riders and drivers
> - Location tracking and mapping
> - Pricing and payments
